Bibimbap Recipe

It is one of the most popular Korean food. "Bibim" means "mixing something" and "bap" means rice.

It can be served with a big bowl or a pot, and we can put many ingredients on the white rice.

Ingredients

  • Spam and a can of tuna
  • Lettuce and bean sprouts
  • Two eggs
  • 2 tbs of go-chu-jang(Korean red)
  • 1 tbs of soy sauce
  • 1 tbs of corn syrup
  • 1 tbs of Korean red pepper
  • 1 tbs of sugar
  • 1/2 tbs of minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbs of sesame oil

Steps

  1. Mix sauce ingredients, including go-chu-jang, soy sauce, corn syrup, Korean red pepper, sugar, minced garlic
  2. Open the can of tuna and mix with the sauce we just made in previous steps
  3. Wash lettuce and bean sprouts
  4. Cook fried eggs
  5. Prepare the big bowl with a white rice
  6. Put lettuce and bean sprouts on the white rice
  7. Put the sauce and sesame oil on the top
