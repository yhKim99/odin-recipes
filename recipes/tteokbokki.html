Tteokbokki Recipe

It is a popular Korean street food. Tteok means rice cake, and bokki means fry and mix.

You can see this food on the street, so you are able to enjoy this food easily.

Ingredients

  • 300g of rice cake
  • Onion and Green onion
  • Fish cake
  • 2 tbs of go-chu-jang
  • 2 tbs of Korean red pepper
  • 2 tbs of sugar
  • 1 tbs of corn syrup
  • 1 tbs of minced garlic
  • 2 tbs of soy sauce

Steps

  1. To make sauce, mix sauce ingredients(go-chu-jang, Korean red pepper, sugar, corn syrup, minced garlic, and soy sauce)
  2. Wash rice cake using cold water
  3. Cut onion, green onion, and fish cake
  4. Prepare 300mL of water and boil it
  5. Once water is boiled, put the rice cake, onion, and fish cake
  6. Put the sauce as well
  7. Once everything is cooked, put the green onion
