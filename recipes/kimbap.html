Kimbap recipe

This is a popular Korean street food. Kim means seaweed, and Bap means rice.

This food is very simple to make. To make it, you can just put all ingredients on the seaweed and roll it.

Ingredients

  • One seaweed
  • Rice with sesame oil
  • Yellow reddish and carrots
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Fried reddish, carrots, and eggs
  2. Put the rice on the seaweed and spread it
  3. Put the reddish, carrots, and eggs
  4. Roll the seaweed
  5. Put the sesame oil on the rolled seaweed
